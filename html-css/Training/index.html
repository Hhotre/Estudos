Restoration Bug

To make the restoration bug you will need 4 items, being those:

  • Head Gear
  • Neck Gear
  • Gloves
  • Ring

Only those can be enchanted with the stronger potions enchantment, other types of gear cannot.
You will need atleast 7% fortification on those items. You can achive that with 40 points on Alchemy

To make this happen you will need two ingredients

  • Cyrodilic Spadetail or Abecean Longfin
    • It can be find at Lake Honrich and at Lake Ilinalta
  • Salt Pile
    • Salt piles are quite common. They can be found in barrels, sacks, and can be bought from many merchants that sell food or alchemy ingredients.
      • A good source of Salt Piles are the docks of Riften, where they occur frequently in Fish Barrels.

After you manage to get at least 25 of those ingredients, you can equipe you gear with the enchantment and produce just one restoration potion, after that you remove you gear, drink the potion, and re-equip you gear, and make another restorarion potion, and repeat, until you get absurd numbers on your gear and potion, just be carefull, going too high may cause the game to crahs
